About Me

I'm Hannah Hamm and I'm a graphic designer. I enjoy creating design, art, food, and social media posts. I enjoy various activities indoor and outdoor. I am currently looking for a full-time graphic design position in the Columbus, OH area. I am a senior graphic design student at Mount Vernon Nazarene University with a minor in general business. I graduate this May.

Design Statement:

Design allows us to make choices that cause us to think differently and react positively. This medium changes how we see and experience the world around us while having the ability to create an encounter to which the viewer can react.

My portfolio is characterized by a unique color palette, bold typography, and a process that dictates the direction for each project. The colors are carefully mixed to ensure a unique color scheme emerges every time. Emphasizing typography is how I establish hierarchy that guides the viewer throughout each piece. These characteristics are revealed through a process that includes generating a design brief, sketching, and ideating. This process helps articulate and execute the needs of my client that allows them to connect with their audience.
